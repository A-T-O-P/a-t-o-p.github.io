Who we are


Project Enlight promotes and provides free quality tutoring for schoolwork and standardized testing for middle and high school students. We strongly believe that through quality education, students will be able to rise up and create a better future for themselves, as well as the rest of society.

Who are the people behind this?


We are a group of motivated students that want to assist those without access to a quality learning environment as a way to further themselves academically and excel in their future careers.
